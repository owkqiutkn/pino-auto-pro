# Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Pino Auto Pro is committed to customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines our procedures for refunds related to vehicle purchases and related services.

## 2. Vehicle Sales

### 2.1 Sold Vehicles
- Vehicle sales are generally final once the purchase agreement is signed and the vehicle is delivered
- Any applicable "cooling-off" or return rights depend on your province's consumer protection laws
- We recommend reviewing your purchase agreement for specific terms

### 2.2 Deposits
- Deposits to hold a vehicle may be refundable or non-refundable as specified at the time of deposit
- Refundable deposits are returned if the sale does not proceed and conditions for refund are met
- Non-refundable deposits may apply for special-order or customized arrangements

## 3. Service and Repair Work

### 3.1 Warranty Repairs
- Warranty repairs are performed at no additional cost as per the warranty terms
- No refund applies for warranty work; repairs are the remedy

### 3.2 Paid Repairs
- Refunds for paid repair work may be considered on a case-by-case basis
- Contact us promptly if you are dissatisfied with repair work

## 4. Extended Warranties and Add-Ons

- Refunds for extended warranties and add-on products (e.g., rust protection, paint protection) are subject to the terms of the specific product
- Contact the administrator of the product or us for eligibility

## 5. Refund Process

To request a refund:
1. Contact us in writing (email or mail) with your name, purchase details, and reason for the request
2. Provide any supporting documentation (e.g., invoice, receipt)
3. Allow up to 10 business days for review and response
4. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method when applicable

## 6. Exceptions

Refunds may not be available for:
- Vehicles sold "as is" where permitted by law
- Normal wear and tear or use of the vehicle after delivery
- Situations where the purchase agreement expressly states no refund
